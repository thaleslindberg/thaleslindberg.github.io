002.

B

This layout exploration shows just a little bit about the brutalist movement through pictures which the user can mouseover and find out more about each of them.

Brutalism is a style with an emphasis on materials, textures and construction, producing highly expressive forms ― which unfortunately are less and less used nowadays.

For those who know me well, know exactly how much I like architecture. In the past I still took a course and over the years I found out that it was just a passion as a hobby but even so, since before entering the course, I admired brutalist architecture a lot and even more its recognition within the movement of contemporary architecture.

Photography by Sean Mungur.
