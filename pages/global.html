GLOBAL IMPACT: TECH FOR GOOD is an innovative event that converges technology, innovation, and creativity, empowering students with a two-week window to address global challenges through coding.

During Tech for Good, students have the opportunity to select from various courses, delving deeper into the challenges within each. Each course presents a unique challenge to tackle.

The distinctive identity of Tech for Good was crafted by Roger Mac, who also conceptualized the desktop version. I was entrusted with the design of the mobile interface.
