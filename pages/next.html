NEXT is a Technology and Innovation Festival that brings together young talents from FIAP presenting their best creations: drones, robots, content for VR devices, mobile applications and much more.

The last NEXT FIAP Festival was in 2017 took place at Casa das Caldeiras, on October 21st.

With things backing to normal right after the pandemic, FIAP is considering to put the festival back in the trails this year. However, the identity is a bit outdated, so we receive the duty to recreate and introduce to our Art Director the new one and this was my result.

I've designed a layout with a free and minimalist aesthetic. So applied a gradient with various pastel colors and a futuristic font to match in with the new identity. This new identity represents all the youth in the students presents at the event.

The students are able to access the drawer menu of the event by hitting the hamburger menu at the top of layout to facilitate even more the use on the website.
